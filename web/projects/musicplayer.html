Hybrid Energy System for Electricity Generation in Khagrachari

  • Technology:Homer software

    • The project's goal was to create a hybrid energy system for Khagrachari's electricity production. To maintain a sustainable and consistent power supply, the system used a combination of solar, wind, and diesel energy sources. Using HOMER software, the system was designed and optimized.

    The project included a number of stages, beginning with determining the community's energy requirements. The design procedure involved choosing the right solar panels, wind turbines, and diesel generators for the hybrid energy system. The team evaluated the system's economic viability, optimized the setup, and performed performance analysis using HOMER software.

    In comparison to the earlier diesel-only system, the final system design showed considerable gains in energy efficiency, economic effectiveness, and sustainability. The effective implementation of this hybrid energy system has contributed to securing a consistent and long-lasting electricity supply for the Khagrachari village.
